## Situation
Growth outpaced process. Data definitions were inconsistent and handoffs were slow, which created forecast risk.

## Action
Unified lifecycle definitions, rebuilt routing logic, and implemented an operating cadence that aligned GTM teams.

## Result
Forecasting inputs became more consistent, and conversion tracking was standardized across teams.
