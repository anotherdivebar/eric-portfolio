## Situation
Multiple teams shipped in parallel without a shared delivery model, which created risk and unclear accountability.

## Action
Built a program framework with milestones, weekly risk reviews, and executive updates.

## Result
Delivery timelines were clearer, with risks surfaced earlier for decision-making.
